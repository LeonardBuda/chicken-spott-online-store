About Us 🎉

Simply the Best™ ✨

Nestled in the heart of Daveyton at 30558 Heald Street 🌍, at the famous Esihlahleni area, 010 Café isn’t just a restaurant—it’s a celebration of township heritage, bold flavours, and unwavering passion! 🔥 Proudly registered as 010 Café (Pty) Ltd 📜, we’ve redefined fast-casual dining by blending tradition with innovation. 🎨

Our Story: Born from Passion, Rooted in Daveyton 🌱

A vision into reality: a space where locals could savour high-quality, affordable meals without compromising on taste or authenticity. 🍗 With our Certificate of Acceptability (DAV/30/2021) 🏅 and a commitment to excellence, we’ve become a culinary landmark—a place where every bite tells a story! 📖

The 010 Experience: Authenticity on Every Plate 🍽️

We craft more than food; we create moments: 🎇

  • Legendary Chicken & Kotas: From succulent ¼ Plates 🍗 to towering Rib Kotas 🥪, each dish honours township recipes. 🌟
  • Innovative Comfort Food: Try our Kream Dala Kream 🧀, Babalazi Chips 🍟, or Proper Dagwoods 🥪—dishes that redefine indulgence! 🎉
  • Farm-to-Table Integrity: Our weighed meats (Ox heart ❤️, Ox liver 🥩, steak 🥩) and sides (pap 🍚, chakalaka 🌶️) ensure freshness in every gram. 🌿

At 010 Café, we don’t just serve meals—we serve memories! 🎶 Come taste why we’re "Simply the Best." 🌟
